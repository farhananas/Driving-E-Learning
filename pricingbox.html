Manual Car (D)

MYR 2000
  • Valid to Drive both Manual and Automatic Transmission Cars while Automatic (DA) License is only valid to drive automatic transmission cars only.
  • Enables student to become a more skillful driver, especially in using the clutch pedal for changing gears which is not found in Automatic transmission cars.
  • Can opt to upgrade to heavy vehicle license, Lorry (E) after 3 years while an Automatic (DA) license holder is not allowed to.
  • Qualified Age for car – 17 years old and above
